Your body is a temple and your health is your wealth The statements may be passe but here at the Lab we believe passionately in these ideas. Offering state-of-the-art gym facilities, a wide range of classes, and dedicated professional personal trainers, we aim to share our love of exercise and a healthy lifestyle with all our clients. All our classes are taught by experienced trainers, with box-fit, spin, tabata, yoga and circuit classes, along with individual weight training and personalised fitness programmes tailored to your personal needs and goals. In addition to training facilities we have a well-stocked shop selling a wide range of personal training equipment which can be bought in store or online. Browse the shop here or view timetables to find a class suitable for you. At the Lab we want to create a cooperative environment where we encourage and support each other. Call into our city centre location to chat to one of us today about starting a programme or joining a class.
